Frequently asked questions
How does the AI scanner work?
Tap AI scan and upload a photo or PDF of any bill, event invitation, or school newsletter. Claude reads it, extracts the key details, and shows you a draft entry to review and edit before saving. Nothing is saved automatically.
How do exam countdowns work?
Add an exam via Add exam. It appears on the priority countdown bar on the calendar. Open the exam to add revision sessions — they show as lilac blocks on the calendar and the exam pill shows a 📚 session count.
Can I print or export the calendar?
Yes — open the Calendar and tap the print icon in the toolbar. Choose Print for a browser print view, or Export PDF to download.
How do I export financial reports?
Open Financial reports from the dashboard or the Reports nav button. Choose your period (month/quarter/year) then tap Export to Excel, Export PDF, or Print from the export bar at the bottom.
How do I change the family currency?
Go to Family Settings → Family profile and change the currency dropdown. This updates across all bills and reports.
How do I add a child account?
Tap Invite member, enter their name, and set the role to Child. Child accounts use PIN-only login — no email needed, perfect for shared tablets.
